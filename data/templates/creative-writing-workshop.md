## Steps

### Step 1: Concept & Theme
- What is the core idea or theme?
- What emotion do you want to evoke?
- What is the central conflict?

### Step 2: World-Building
- Setting: Where and when?
- Rules: What is possible in this world?
- Atmosphere: What does it feel, look, sound like?

### Step 3: Character Development
For each major character:
- Name, role, motivation
- Internal conflict
- Arc: How do they change?
- Voice: How do they think/speak?

### Step 4: Narrative Structure
- **Hook:** Opening that grabs attention
- **Rising Action:** Building tension
- **Climax:** The turning point
- **Falling Action:** Consequences unfold
- **Resolution:** How it ends

### Step 5: Craft & Polish
- Show, don't tell
- Sensory details
- Pacing and rhythm
- Dialogue authenticity

## Verification Checklist
- [ ] Is the theme clear but not heavy-handed?
- [ ] Are characters multi-dimensional?
- [ ] Does the plot have tension and stakes?
- [ ] Is the setting vivid and immersive?
- [ ] Is the ending satisfying (even if ambiguous)?
